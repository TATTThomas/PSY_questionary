Strobe Goggles


A R&D tool for vision training by Senaptec ( https://www.senaptec.com/product/senaptec-strobe-standard). It could reform or strengthen users motor function, body balance and reaction time with rapidly blocking users eyesight at a fixed frequency. As the lenses flicker on and off, it is harder to process information, which helps the brain focus on visual tasks, which provides an edge for athletes to perform at a higher level more consistently and with better decision making.
