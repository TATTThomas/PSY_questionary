Game Day Plan


Game day plan means to finely schedule every details during the game day, including pre-night warm up, transportation, diets, the pre-game exercise and game syllabus. Even setting plans like 1 to 2 days before game will be a huge advantage for players to get into the zone more quickly with better inner-states.

Notice:

  1. Knowing yourself, make appropriate arrangements depends on own circumstances.
  2. Collect more relevant information before planning to increase the sense of control and avoid unpredictable chaos.
  3. Last but not least, a great feast and self rewards after game are always important.
